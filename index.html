Directions by Car

From the north - take junction 5 off the M25 onto the A21 towards Hastings, take a left off at the A228 and head towards the Hop Farm, continue for 3.8 miles, straight over at the roundabout and then take the 2nd right after the railway arch into the venue site.

From the south - turn onto the A21 towards London, turn off at the A228 and head towards the Hop Farm, continue for 3.8 miles, straight over at the roundabout and then take the 2nd right after the railway arch into the venue site.

Directions by public transport

Trains running from London Charing Cross, London Bridge or Victoria to Paddock Wood take approximately 40 minutes. From there the venue is less than 5 minutes away by taxi.

Weather / dress code

Our wedding is a festival/country inspired day; so please wear funky footwear! Wellies, boots and sandals are more than welcome. Ladies, heels are an option but perhaps steer clear of the skyscrapers! We would also advise bringing coats and jackets just in case!

Dietary requirements
